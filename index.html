about

I am years old, born in Iran and raised in England. I am studying Part III of the Maths Tripos at Cambridge, taking mainly combinatorics courses and writing an essay on Canonical Ramsey Theory. I enjoy watching films, playing games, solving puzzles and travelling. I was the Webmaster for CUPLC from 2023–2024 and the Treasurer for the Poker Society from 2024–2025.

I rowed both at school and for Fitzwilliam College Boat Club (only for one term at the latter). I competed in the 2024 May Bumps and won blades. We had the pleasure of bumping Trinity out of Division 1, leaving them with no boats in the top division for the first time in history. I capsized a double during a race at Marlow Spring Regatta in 2019. I have competed in powerlifting a few times, breaking a regional record in the −74kg u18 deadlift in 2022. My best deadlift is 220kg at a bodyweight of 73kg. I am yet to bench 2 plates.
